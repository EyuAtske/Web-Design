About Us

Porsche is one of the most iconic luxury car manufacturers in the world, renowned for its precision engineering, performance, and timeless design. Founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche in Stuttgart, Germany, the brand originally focused on automotive development and consulting before producing its own vehicles. The company’s first car, the Porsche 356, debuted in 1948 and quickly set the tone for what would become a legacy of innovation, blending lightweight construction with powerful engines and superb handling.
Over the years, Porsche has continued to refine its reputation for excellence with legendary models like the 911, introduced in 1964. The 911 remains a symbol of sports car perfection, evolving through decades while maintaining its distinctive shape and rear-engine layout. Beyond sports cars, Porsche expanded its lineup to include SUVs such as the Cayenne and Macan, and the luxury four-door Panamera, broadening its appeal while maintaining its performance heritage. This balance of luxury, power, and practicality has helped Porsche attract a loyal global fan base.
In recent years, Porsche has embraced the future of mobility through electrification and sustainability. The introduction of the fully electric Taycan marked a major step toward combining cutting-edge technology with Porsche’s racing DNA. Despite moving into the electric era, Porsche remains dedicated to its founding principles — delivering driving pleasure, exceptional craftsmanship, and innovation. Whether on the racetrack or the open road, Porsche continues to embody the spirit of performance and engineering excellence that has defined it for nearly a century.
